History Of Sheda House

Sheda House was birth in 2018 but did not commence business until 2019. Sheda House is designed to be a home for creatives in all areas. we aim to build a community where you can come and pick up skills that would help you express your creativity and earn an income It is a creative space that houses Toluade photography FireSwitch Tech digital agency and Cypher Power system

Founders

Timothy Ayodele (co-founder)

Timothy Ayodele, a software enginner/CEO FireSwitch Technologies, is a full-stack Developer,who has hands-on experience with PHP, React, Javascript and BlockChain developnment.

Toluade Ayodele (co-founder)

Ayodele Tolu is a documentary and wedding photographer based in Ibadan, Nigeria. She focuses on the common man revealing their interests, personality and happenings around their lives that is often neglected, thus revealing that every man is more than what meets the eyes.

Our Culture

We are building a community which understands that the world is changing and that we need to prepare for the changing world. We understand that those who join the train early are going to be the first to eat from the pie when the word finally realises it. We train and also allow working with the team as interns.In sheas house we share close bonds as friends and as community, you are welcome to join the sheda family
